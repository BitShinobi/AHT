Advanced Game-theory to incentavize price appreciation

People buying and holding are essential for good price performance for any asset. The AHT project incentivizes people to hold for extended periods of time by rewarding them with extra tokens and penalizes those who sell early. But incentivizing with extra tokens is being done in such a way as to still keep the token non-inflationary. Here's how it works: those who sell early are penalized through a 10% tax. Half of the tax is given to long-term holders and shillers, and the remaining half is burned. Yes, AHT is deflationary! So, with time, the total number of Attention Harvesting Tokens decreases, thus reducing the supply and making it more valuable. This mechanism ensures that shillers and long-term holders are rewarded with bonus tokens while still keeping the token deflationary!

Fully transparent, decentralized and secure

The attention harvesting token project employs a technology called decentralized immutable smart contracts to guarantee absolute security and transparency. These smart contracts have no admin keys, meaning there's no single entity with control over them. Instead, they rely on a distributed network to maintain and verify transactions. Moreover, all the code used in these smart contracts will be accessible to the public, ensuring transparency. This means that anyone, at any time, can examine the code to ensure it's functioning as intended and not engaging in any fraudulent activity. Consequently, the risk of scams or unethical behavior within the project is effectively eliminated.
